Xbox Series X Rockets Ahead In Sales

If you weren’t quick on the draw, you likely missed out on preordering an Xbox Series X or Series S. Preorders for the new Xbox consoles began at 11 a.m. Eastern on Tuesday, 11/10/2020, but most of the major retailers sold out in minutes. Some people said they were unable to preorder the console because the Microsoft website was down at the most inopportune time.

Xbox said Tuesday afternoon it was “humbled by the record-breaking demand for Xbox Series X and Series S.”, “Huge thanks to everyone for the excitement,”. Xbox then tweeted, “If you weren’t successful today be sure to sign up with retailers for updates, and expect more consoles to be available on November 10. Some lucky people were able to snag an Xbox preorder on Nov. 10., but it was once again sold out in minutes.

As Microsoft even said themselves... "Thanks to you, we had our largest launch day in Xbox history. More people are playing than ever before, and what’s better, we’re playing together. We can’t wait for you to keep showing us the power of play."
