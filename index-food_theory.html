Matthew Robert Patrick

YouTuber

MatPats Food Theory

The third Theory channel, and fourth overall; The Food Theorists began releasing videos in July 2020. It came at the tail end of multiple cryptic teases Patrick on his Twitter account. As the name suggests, The Food Theorists, using the same style as its predecessors, blends food science with psychology, physiology, and conspiracy. Theorist Media reports that the channel has been in the works for two years. Videos that illustrate the direction of the channel include 'How to SURVIVE Spicy Food!' and 'Chuck E Cheese Pizza, Should You Be Scared?'; the former conducting tests on how to mitigate capsaicin (inspired by the show Hot Ones) and the latter debunking the myth shared virally via social media that the pizza chain Chuck E. Cheese serves its uneaten pizza slices from previous customers to other ones. Currently, The Food Theorists has 1.94 Million Subscribers and 70 million views. It had reached 1 million subscribers exactly a week after launch.

At first it sounds like a weird concept, especially for somebody who theories about video games & films, but watch one or two of his Food Theory videos & you'll be hooked. It's a fun way to bust myths & learn about what you eat all at the same time!
